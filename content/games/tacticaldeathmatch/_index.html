A Fortnite game.

Tactical Deathmatch is a free-for-all deathmatch with high damaging weapons and a specifically designed map to let your aim shine, as you race to be the first to gain 30 eliminations. Select from 12 primary and 5 secondary weapons and find your way to victory among 19 other players.
