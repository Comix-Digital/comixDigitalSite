THIS WILL PROBABLY BE THE LAST UPDATE OF THIS MOD!!
- Added copper tools and armor
  - Copper tools and armor can oxidize, going through the 3 other states present in vanilla Minecraft
  - Copper tools and armor can be waxed by combining the desired armor/tool piece with honeycomb on a crafting table
  - Something REALLY COOL happens to unwaxed copper tools when lightning strikes nearby
- Added amethyst tools and armor, which make really cool amethyst sounds when used
- Made leather tools dyeable, but they cannot be mixed dyed unfortunately
- Updated modloader to NeoForge 1.21.1
