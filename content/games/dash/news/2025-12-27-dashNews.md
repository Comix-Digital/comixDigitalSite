Hello, everyone!

I needed to make a new version of the game in response to the Unity security vulnerability that was discovered on June 4, 2025, so this is now the final release, unless another vulnerability is found.

New:

-Ability to customize the colour of you cube. Press on "COLOUR" in the main menu and use the colour picker, enter a hex code, or type in a basic colour name.

-Controller is now supported on (hopefully) all platforms.

Changes and Bugfixes:

-All UI is now anchored properly and will look correct on all devices (for the most part)

-Walls made of black cubes on Level 10 will no longer be floating on most of the top paths and one middle path

-The jump button will no longer also make you move right on touch controls

This is the most likely final version of Dash.

Until next game,

-TheFirstComix
