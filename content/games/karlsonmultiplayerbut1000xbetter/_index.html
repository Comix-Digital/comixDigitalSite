Karlson Multiplayer is a great game but missing one thing... More guns!

Just the pistol is really boring, so this mod contains 3 additional guns.

Guns:
  • Pistol
Already in the game, just slightly buffed.
  • Uzi
Straight from the original game, but with changed stats to avoid being overpowered.
  • Shotgun
Straight from the original game, but with changed stats to avoid being overpowered.
  • Banagnum
A one-shot, hand cannon/deagle-type thing. The model is by SteD.
